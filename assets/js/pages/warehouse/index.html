OUR STORY

INDUSTRIAL SOUL.
PREMIUM SPIRIT.

The Warehouse Food and Bar is Port Harcourt's premier destination for those who seek more than just a night out. Located at the heart of Odili Road, we've redefined the resto-lounge experience by blending industrial aesthetics with high-end luxury.

Whether you're hitting the pins in our state-of-the-art bowling alley, savoring artisanal cocktails at the bar, or enjoying a signature meal in our lounge — we provide an atmosphere that is both electric and refined. It's not just a venue. It's an experience.
