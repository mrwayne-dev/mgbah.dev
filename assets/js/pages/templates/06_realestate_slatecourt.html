FAQs

Frequently
asked questions.

How do you verify your listings?
Every property is physically inspected, the title is searched at the Land Registry, and we confirm the seller or landlord's authority before listing. We do not list properties we have not personally vetted.
Do you handle diaspora clients?
Yes — about 40% of our clients live abroad. We handle virtual tours, video walkthroughs, secure deposit collection via Paystack, and end-to-end purchase or rental on your behalf.
What is your commission structure?
For sales: 5% on the agreed sale price. For lettings: one month's rent, capped at 10% of annual rent. No hidden fees, no agency fee on top of the holding deposit.
Can you manage my property if I'm abroad?
Yes. Our property management package starts at ₦180,000/month and covers tenant vetting, rent collection, monthly reporting, maintenance coordination, and an annual property inspection.
What payment methods do you accept?
Bank transfer (NGN, USD, GBP), Paystack, Flutterwave, and escrow for high-value transactions. We do not accept cash.
