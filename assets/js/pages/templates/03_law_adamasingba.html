Frequently Asked

The questions we hear most.

How much does a consultation cost?
Initial consultations are ₦25,000 for thirty minutes, applied as credit toward any retainer. We are transparent about pricing because trust starts there.
Do you handle matters outside Lagos?
Yes. We hold practice rights nationwide and have appeared at courts in Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, and the Supreme Court. Most client meetings are now hybrid.
How quickly can you take on urgent matters?
For genuine emergencies — injunctions, interim relief, time-bound filings — we can mobilise within twenty-four hours. WhatsApp our duty partner directly.
Do you offer monthly retainers for small businesses?
Yes. Our SME retainer starts at ₦150,000/month and includes contract review, regulatory monitoring, and a monthly check-in call. Designed for founders who don't want to think about legal until they need to.
